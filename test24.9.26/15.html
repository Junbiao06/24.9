关于2024年下半年全国英语等级考试学生相关注意事项的通知

2024年下半年全国英语等级考试将于9月21日举行。笔试上午9:00开始，口试下午14:00开始。 笔试考生8:30开始入场，8:45后迟到考生禁止进场；口试考生13:30开始进入候考室， 14:00后迟到考生禁止进场。语音室将于9月18日、9月20日的10:00-16:00在仙林校区播放音频 ，供考生对听力耳机进行检测，本次考试我校将采用调频语音设备进行听力播放，波段：FM73.1。 考生考试当天务必再次确认自己考试用的耳机是否状态正常，学校不提供考生用的备用耳机和备用电池。 听力考试过程中，考生必须全程佩戴耳机，若考生听力考试时不戴耳机，非听力考试时间佩戴耳机，均按违规处理。

The National English Proficiency Test for the second half of 2024 is scheduled to take place on September 21st. The written examination will commence at 9:00 AM, followed by the oral test at 2:00 PM. Candidates for the written test are required to arrive for admission starting from 8:30 AM, and those arriving after 8:45 AM will not be permitted entry.Candidates may enter the waiting room from 1:30 PM onwards. However, candidates who arrive late after 2:00 PM will not be allowed to participate in the oral test.To facilitate listening comprehension practice, audio materials will be played between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM on September 18th and September 20th at Xianlin Campus. For this examination, our school will utilize FM voice equipment with a frequency band set at FM73.1.On the day of the test, it is essential that candidates ensure their headphones are in proper working condition as spare headphones and batteries will not be provided by the school. Throughout the listening test section, candidates must wear their headphones continuously; failure to do so during this specific period or wearing them during non-listening sections would constitute a violation.
